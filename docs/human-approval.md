# Human Approval

Human approval is mandatory for high-impact or irreversible actions.

## Default Approval Triggers

- Sending external email or messages
- Destructive edits or deletes
- Financial or compliance-affecting actions
- Any physical-world execution beyond observation/recommendation

## Approval Record

At minimum, log:

- Requested action token
- Rationale and expected outcome
- Approver identity and timestamp
- Decision (approved/rejected)
